The Problem

A Changing Climate in South Sudan

South Sudan is increasingly affected by climate change, including flooding, drought, deforestation, and unpredictable weather patterns.

These changes damage agriculture, reduce food production, and threaten livelihoods, especially in rural communities.

Daily Habits

Actionable Tips for Everyone

Stop Waste Burning

Dispose of household waste properly instead of burning it to keep the air clean and reduce toxic emissions.

Conserve Water

During unpredictable weather patterns, saving clean water ensures your community has enough during dry spells.

Share Knowledge

Talk to your neighbors about climate changes. Community awareness is the first step toward resilience.
